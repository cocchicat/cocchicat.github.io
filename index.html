Biography

Cocchi is a 5-year old Siberian cat from the Angel of Siberia cattery in South Seattle. As a kitten, she was first named Angela after the head of the cattery, before she was re-named Cocchi after the manga character from Chi's Sweet Home. A brown tabby cat with clear green eyes, Cocchi has received high praise for her reverent and stunning beauty, capturing has the hearts of the nation with her charming visage.

Favorite food: Minnow
Favorite season: Winter
Favorite sleeping place: Cat tree
Personality type: ISTP
